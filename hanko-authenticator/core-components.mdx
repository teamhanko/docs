## Hanko Authenticator apps

To allow your users to conveniently manage their private keys, they need a device that can sign authentication requests on your request. Such a device is called an authenticator, and could be a computer, a smartphone, or a second-factor device like a USB stick.

To facilitate the usage of the Hanko service, Hanko offers production-ready authenticator apps for Android and iOS that can be used with your applications. By simply touching the device's fingerprint sensor or using facial recognition with biometric cameras, your users can securely log into your applications. Read more about the [Hanko Authenticator apps](/hanko-authenticator/guides/authenticator-apps) or start with the [getting started guide](/hanko-authenticator/quickstart/authenticator-app).

## Hanko FIDO UAF Client SDKs & plugins

In order to make integration of on-device FIDO UAF authentication into your existing native app as easy as possible, Hanko offers a mobile client SDK for both Android and iOS. Read more in the [SDKs section](/hanko-authenticator/sdks/android).

If you use Keycloak as your identity and access management solution, you can integrate the Hanko Authenticator (FIDO UAF) as a login option through the [Keycloak plugin](/hanko-authenticator/sdks/keycloak-plugin).
