title: FAQ
description: Collection of common questions that Hanko users get.
---

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Is Hanko open-source?">
Yes, Hanko is open-source.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is Hanko Cloud?">
  Hanko Cloud provides a hosted Hanko backend, simplifying the initiation and
  management of new Hanko projects. It comes with out-of-the-box user management
  and analytics.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I self-host Hanko?">
  Yes, you can self-host. Please refer to this
  [guide](https://github.com/teamhanko/hanko/tree/main/backend#hanko-backend).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can I integrate Hanko to my app?">
  Check out our [quickstart guides](/quickstarts/fullstack/next) to help you
  integrate Hanko with your favorite frameworks.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I integrate Hanko to a mobile app?">
  Yes, you can. However, we currently do not have SDKs for Swift, Kotlin, React
  Native, or Flutter. These are on our roadmap for future development. In the
  meantime, you can make your mobile client work directly with the Hanko API,
  but it's a bit more work on your end.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I add the passkey to my existing auth?">
  Yes, you can. Refer to our [Passkey API docs](/passkey-api/introduction) for more
  information.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="My question is not listed here">
Join our [Discord Community](https://www.hanko.io/community) and feel free to ask any questions you might have there.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
